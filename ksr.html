Apply for Leave :

  1. Taking Leave more than two days requireds leave form with proper reason.
  2. Attendence percentage must be above 80% in individual subjects to attend the end semester exam.
  3. Should bring Medical Certificate if the number of leave taken is more than three days.

Apply for ON-Duty or Perimission :

  1. For any work inside the KSREI campus apply for Perimission.
  2. For any activity outside the KSREI campus apply for On_Duty.
  3. To apply On-Duty should attach the proper flyer or invitation of the event and the payment details of the corresponding event.
  4. Sould apply for On-Duty before two days from the date of event.
